About RGMCET

Welcome to Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering & Technology

Established in 1995, RGMCET is one of the premier engineering institutions in Andhra Pradesh, providing excellent technical education and research opportunities. Our college is affiliated with JNTUA and accredited by NAAC and NBA. We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, fostering an environment of innovation, excellence, and continuous improvement.

Our institution is committed to empowering students with knowledge, practical skills, and the confidence to excel in their chosen fields, and we have a proven track record of producing professionals who contribute significantly to the industry and society at large.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, promoting the advancement of knowledge and producing globally competent engineers who can contribute meaningfully to the development of society.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education and create an environment of continuous learning and innovation. We aim to nurture creativity, integrity, and professional ethics in our students, empowering them to meet the challenges of the modern world.

Our Core Values

Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do, from academic programs to research and community outreach.

Innovation: Encouraging creativity and innovative thinking to develop new technologies and solutions.

Integrity: Promoting honesty, fairness, and ethical behavior in all aspects of education and research.

Inclusivity: Fostering a diverse and inclusive campus community, where everyone is respected and valued.
