Courses Offered at RGMCET

Computer Science Engineering

The Computer Science Engineering program focuses on the theory, design, development, and application of computer systems and software. Students will learn programming, data structures, algorithms, operating systems, database management, and networking.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

This program encompasses the study of electronic devices, circuits, communication systems, and signal processing. Students will gain knowledge in areas such as analog and digital electronics, microprocessors, embedded systems, and telecommunication technologies.

Electrical and Electronic Engineering

The Electrical and Electronic Engineering course covers a broad range of topics, including electrical circuits, power systems, control systems, and electronics. Students will develop skills in analyzing and designing electrical systems for various applications.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. Students will learn about structural engineering, environmental engineering, geotechnics, and project management.

Mechanical Engineering

This program involves the study of mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and fluid dynamics. Students will learn how to design, analyze, and manufacture mechanical systems and understand the principles of energy conversion and machinery operation.
